Muhammad Ashfaq

Muhammad received his bachelor's degree in information technology from University of Gujrat, Paksitan and his master's degree in computer science and technology from Jiangsu University, China. Currently, he is a research fellow at the Digital Industry Center - Software Engineering Unit, Fondazione Bruno Kessler (FBK) and a doctoral student at the department of Information Engineering and Computer Science University of Trento, Italy.

His current research interests include: Software testing (testing software product lines, complex parameteric systems, random testing, combinatorial testing), AI for testing, testing for AI, and web technologies.
